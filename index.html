Why choose Germany for your medical studies?

In Germany, most of the Universities are state funded and hence the only costs involved are the processes before getting a license. Not only that, you could start receiving a stipend within a year of moving here. Merit is the only criteria for admission & there is no management seat or favoring involved. According to German law, universities are not permitted to discriminate against or grant preferential treatment to persons on basis of race, ethnic group, gender, or social class.

Germany has the top University clinics in the world, known for its specialised doctors, training and research. Cost of living is about 10000â‚¬ per year and can be easily earned back in a few months. You can enjoy extremely good standards of living and make friends and acquaintances from across the world. There are great job prospects as there is an acute scarcity of doctors. Obtaining a permanent residency and citizenship is not as difficult as the other EU countries as well. It is also very easy to travel to other EU countries with your schengen visa.
