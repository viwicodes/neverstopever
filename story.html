Our Story

Here's how we reached here in Germany and made it out here.

Aniket and I came to Germany to do our masters in computer science in 2013, the process was quite easy, our course was taught in English and it was pretty straightforward to find jobs. Since this worked so well for us and engineers in general, I wanted my sister also to come to Germany for her studies. She was pursuing her MBBS and we looked up the prospects of her doing her further studies in India as well as other countries like USA, UK and Europe.

It was either too expensive , too competitive that she might have to give exams year after year to find a seat and it might not have been in the field of her choice .

We looked up Germany for doctors and there was not enough information on the internet , the process was not clear and it meant quite an investment in terms of language and time . But looking at the advantages with respect to getting the specialization of her choice with v little investment of time and money. We decided she would pursue her career in Germany . She worked on her German skills while I deep dived into the process , spoke to several people in medicine and in the offices that granted licenses , understood the process and applied for her. She got through the whole process smoothly within a few months and is now working as a cardiologist here.

It was the same to bring her fianc√© and 3-4 friends who learnt about the process and decided to come here and are doing well in various branches of medicine. Several doctors have been very happy with our assistance since the last 3 years. We decided to expand and do this more professionally and bring in more people from foreign countries and help them achieve their dream of becoming doctors in the most effective way.

Here's our story in a nutshell.

  • With aspirations of studying in Germany, Aniket and I embarked on a journey to make our dreams come true. Luckily, the process was easy breezy.
  • When everything was coming together to make that dream a reality, my sister came into the picture with her MBBS degree. She was yearning for further studies but facing hauntingly expensive costs or stiff competition elsewhere in places such as the USA, UK, and India.
  • Our solution? A chance at unlocking her academic potential overseas whilst exploring a new culture...in beautiful Germany!
  • But, After an extensive search for doctors in Germany, we weren't finding enough information online and the process seemed far too complicated. As she worked to enhance her German language proficiency, I immersed myself in the life-changing process for so many.
  • Through conversations with healthcare practitioners and offices who issue licenses, my understanding deepened as we applied for hers. Within a few months our efforts paid off; she is now working proudly as a cardiologist here!
  • Our success extended outwards too - her fianc√© plus a few other friends were similarly inspired by what they had learnt of this pathway and arrived safely in Germany shortly after.
  • Our assistance has kept many doctors happy over these past few years that our ambitions have reached new heights. It is time now for us to expand professionally and bring more aspirants from overseas even closer towards their dream job. We look forward to winning them one step up the ladder every inch along its way!
